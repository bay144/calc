Designing Labels That Actually Hold Up

A binder or envelope label only has one job: be readable at a glance. Keep names short — "Groceries" over "Weekly Grocery Budget Fund" — and pick a style that matches how the rest of your binder is organized, so categories feel like one cohesive system instead of a pile of mismatched notes.

Printing Your Sheet
