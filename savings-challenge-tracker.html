How These Challenges Work

The $1,000 Fund challenge breaks a round savings goal into 10 equal $100 milestones — useful as a starter emergency fund before building toward a full 3-6 month target. The 52-Week Challenge instead ramps up gradually, from $1 in week one to $52 in week fifty-two, for a total of $1,378 by the end of the year. Tap any tile in any order — the tracker just totals up what you've marked as done.

Once you've hit a milestone here, the Emergency Fund Runway Calculator can show you what a full 3, 6, or 9-month fund looks like for your actual household expenses, and the Binder Planner can help you set up a physical system to keep the momentum going.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 52-Week Savings Challenge work?

Each week you save an increasing amount — $1 in week one, $2 in week two, and so on up to $52 in week fifty-two — for a total of $1,378 by the end of the year. Some people run it in reverse, saving the largest amounts first while motivation is high.

Do I have to follow the exact challenge order?

No — click any tile in any order that fits your budget that week. The tracker just adds up whatever you've marked as filled, so it's fine to save a $52 week during a flush month and a $1 week during a tight one.

Does the tracker save my progress?

Yes — your filled tiles are saved automatically in this browser, so your progress is still there next time you visit from the same device and browser.
