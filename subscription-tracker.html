Ledgr Subscriptions

A small browser extension for a small annoyance: subscriptions that quietly renew while you're not paying attention. Add what you're paying for, and Ledgr Subscriptions keeps a running total of your true monthly cost — streaming normalized alongside yearly software licenses and that gym membership billed every 90 days — and reminds you before the charge hits.

Everything is entered by hand and stored only in your browser. No account, no inbox scanning, no data leaving your machine — the same no-signup philosophy as every calculator on this site.

What it does

Add a subscription with its name, amount, and billing cycle (weekly, monthly, yearly, or a custom interval), and it's added to a sorted list showing what renews next. A running total up top converts every cycle into a monthly-equivalent figure, so you can see your real recurring spend at a glance. A daily check flags anything renewing today and badges the toolbar icon when something's coming up within three days.
